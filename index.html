Artist bio

Daniel J. Hagenbuch was born in Philadelphia, PA. His first interest in Art was drawing comics and animation as a boy. Daniel started to study Fine Art as a teenager. Pursued Art studies in Portraiture, Figurative art, Landscapes, Etchings and Stone Sculpture.

Andrew Lojszczyk

Studied under Famous Polish Artist Andrew Lojszczyk, Andrew was a Fine Artist and Restoration Expert in Poland. Andrew was a Professor of Fine Art and Art Restoration at the Academy of Art, Warsaw, Poland. Andrew was the Art Restoration Expert for the San Diego Mission Church De Alcala 1989-1997.

classically trained

Daniel studied classical Fine Art and Art theory under mentor Andrew Lojszczyk for two years 1994–1997 as a student Apprentice, helping Andrew in some painting project commissions.

education

He also studied at various colleges in San Diego and Los Angeles, CA. Daniel has shown his work at various galleries on the east and west coast. Was a member of the San Diego Portrait Society 2011– 2014.

Associate of Arts, Bucks Bounty Community College, Newtown PA

Bachelor of Arts, Philadelphia University, Philadelphia PA
